# Security Policy

Responsible disclosure

If you discover a potential vulnerability, please email security@example.com with details. Do not post vulnerabilities publicly.

Guidelines

- Provide steps to reproduce, affected versions, and impact
- Include suggested mitigations if possible
- We will acknowledge receipt and respond according to severity

Data handling and privacy

Follow documented privacy practices in docs/15_AI_PROMPTS.md and docs/18_SECURITY.md.
