# NeuroSpark AI

> Discover your potential through AI-powered challenges, evidence-based assessments, and continuous personal growth.

## Vision

NeuroSpark AI is an intelligent platform designed to help people discover and develop their strengths through practical challenges, cognitive assessments, and long-term progress tracking.

Unlike traditional personality tests, NeuroSpark continuously learns from user interactions to build a richer understanding of their abilities.

---

## Core Principles

- Evidence over assumptions
- Continuous learning
- Privacy first
- AI as a coach, not a judge
- Personal growth over labels
